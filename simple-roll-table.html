Rollable Tables for RPGs

Table 1: Treasure Chests

Roll on this table to determine what treasure is in the chest:

Roll Treasure
1-2 50 gold pieces
3-4 Small gem worth 100 gold pieces
5 Scroll of Cure Wounds
6 Magic Ring (roll on Table 2)

Table 2: Magic Rings

Roll on this table to determine the type of magic ring:

Roll Magic Ring
1-2 Ring of Protection (+1 AC)
3-4 Ring of Fire Resistance
5 Ring of Invisibility
6 Ring of Spell Storing (1st level spell)
